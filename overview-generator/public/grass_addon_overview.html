mundialis GRASS GIS Addons Manual pages

GRASS GIS is free software, anyone may develop his/her own extensions (addons). The addons listed here are currently not part of the core software package.
Addons can easily be installed in your local GRASS GIS installation through the graphical user interface (Menu - Settings - Addons Extension - Install) or via the g.extension command.

These manual pages are updated daily. Last run: 21 Jun 2024

How to contribute?

See instructions here: How to create a GRASS GIS addon.

General

  • g.extension.github: GRASS GIS Addon to download and install, remove or update extensions (addons) from the official GRASS GIS Addons repository. [testsuite: yes]
  • g.region.area: GRASS GIS addon to compute the area in sqm and compare it with a given maximum [testsuite: yes]
  • g.message.stdout: GRASS GIS module to write a message to stdout [testsuite: yes]
  • g.region.bbox: openEO related GRASS GIS addons - Sets the region from a bounding box and CRS for that bbox. [testsuite: no]

Temporal
